Our Mission
Get a headstart on your future.

It's in the name. At the Storyboard Initiative, we don't just help students plan out their careers, we help them envision their careers.

Our goal is to provide and resource young minds with the correct resources and tools for success. We implement this through the use of numerous programs, initiatives, and cirriculums. We firmly believe that all students deserve the same oppotunities for success - that's why all our programs are completely free and readily accessible.
