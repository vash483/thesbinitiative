Our Mission

Educate

Using professional insight from various industry professionals, DECA alumni, and management boards, TSI serves to create efficient and effective educational programs to prepare young minds for their futures in business.

Resource

Our program aims to provide career-learning opportunities for everyone. With the use of frequent fundraisers and partnership programs, and community donations, we plan to fund our career mentorship programs for underpriveleged communities across the Bay Area.

Inspire

With the use of our courses, seminars, fairs, and workshops we aim to inspire students to use the opportunities and resources they have been given to take their futures to the next level.
